Peanut Butter and Jelly

Description

A favorite that has been finding it way into kids lunch bags for years. It is as easy to make as it is delicious.

Ingredients

  • White Bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly(grape or strawberry

Steps

  1. Spread Peanut butter on one slice of bread
  2. Spread jelly on the other slice of bread
  3. Combine bread pieces to form the sandwich
  4. Optional- cut off crust
